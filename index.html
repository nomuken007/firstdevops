DevOps enables

DevOps is a cultural shift to bring greater collaboration between multiple teams, products, IT, and operations. DevOps emphasise frequent deploying and testing of software in a secure and reliable method through automation. In a DevOps model, development and operation are no longer working in “siloed”. DevOps uses technology stack which aids in the provisioning of the infrastructure to enable the testing of applications.
